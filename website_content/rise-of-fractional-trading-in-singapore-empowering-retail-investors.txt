        h3 : What Is Fractional Trading? 
        p : Fractional trading allows investors to own less than one whole share of a company. Instead of being restricted to buying shares in lots of 1,000 or 100, investors can now invest in a fraction of a share – for a fraction of the cost. This democratizes investing, as no stock is too expensive to consider.
