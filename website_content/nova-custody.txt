  p : By replacing legacy systems with our comprehensive cloud-enabled post trade settlement solution, custodians can reduce operational cost, increase Straight-Through-Processing (STP) rates, and provide an extensive user-experience for their clients while increasing operational efficiencies and focus on key business objectives.
NOVA Custody provides functionality specific to brokers providing custodial services to trading (and non-trading) clients.  This includes automatic position keeping and reporting, calculation of service fees according to flexible rules, periodic production of composite custody statements, automatic adjustments for Corporate Actions (e.g. dividends, bonus issues, and re-constructions). 
  h2 : Features 
  div:
   h5 : Stock Holding Maintenance 
   p : Provides a real time view of all Stock Positions for clients who are defined as being custody clients including separation of Pending and Confirmed Holdings within segregated categories of custody client, firm’s principal and firm’s clearing. Positions are automatically adjusted for Stock Movements generated from various functional modules within the system such as Trade, Settlement, Custody, Corporate Action, Stock Lending and Margin Trading. 
  div:
   h5 : Stock Movement, Stock Account, Stock Projection Enquiry 
   p : Provides a history of movements for subsequent enquiry by Instrument, client and firms; a summary of the global stock position by security; and real time list of Projected Stock Position by instrument and custodian for a user-defined period in the future. 
  div:
   h5 : Stock Transfer and Custodian Transfer 
   p : NOVA provides Stock Transfer functions including Deposit, Withdrawal, Client Transfer, Custodian Transfer, Mass Withdrawal, and Stock Earmarking. These functions allow for the Stock Transaction to be ‘Immediate’ or ‘Pending’ based on customer’s requirement.
